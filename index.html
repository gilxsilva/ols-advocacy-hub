What is Operation Lone Star?

Operation Lone Star (OLS) was launched by Texas Governor Greg Abbott in March 2021. It deploys law enforcement resources to the Texas-Mexico border, ostensibly to address border crossing and smuggling.

The operation has drawn criticism for its militarized approach, constitutional concerns, and humanitarian impacts on migrants and border communities.

With a price tag exceeding $11 billion and counting, many are questioning whether this massive expenditure is producing any meaningful results beyond political theatrics.

Texas has spent more on Operation Lone Star than the annual budgets of 20 U.S. states. Source: U.S. Census Bureau, Texas State Budget Reports
